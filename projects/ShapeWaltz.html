Shape Waltz

Year: 2024 | Medium: 3D Animation

Project Summary

The Shape Waltz is a simple 3D Animation set to a score (click here to listen on Youtube). The waltz consists of three distinctive geometric animations matching the three aspects of the score: waltz (blue ball), melody (ball waterfall), and bass (orange cube).

It was a fun challenge to animate directly to a score, and definitely a good exercise for my first time animating a larger project. See images below for behind the scenes photos.
