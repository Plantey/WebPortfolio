Wishing Well

Year: 2025 | Medium: Video Game

Project Summary

Wishing Well is a short bit.ly game about a magic wishing fountain in the middle of town. The player makes their way around a small map in order to obtain 1 or more coins to use at the well. There is charming dialogue, silly characters, and some unique art throughout the game.

This game was developed with the Bit.ly engine, a free, online, in-browser game engine tool, that is perfect for creating small game projects. It was my first attempt at a project using this engine, and I had a lot of funworking within its constraints.
