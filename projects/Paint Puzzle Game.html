Paint Puzzle Game

Year: 2025 | Medium: Video Game

Project Summary

The Paint Puzzle Game is a simple 2D platformer. I was a co-creater of this project, along with Nicole Halman (Artist) and Gulio Mazzotta (Programmer). I played the role of designer; I handled level design, UI and menu design, and kept our group organized and on track to meet our deadline.

I contributed to the level design of the project. Creating a comprehensive level design document (See images below) that maps out progression for mechanics in the game. Balancing tutorial levels, challenging puzzles, and relaxing and fun levels all into a neat sequence. After some playtesting, the levels were further refined into their final states. In the images section below you can view my drawn paper prototypes for each level.

I handled the UI skeleton map, the UI elements list, the mockups for each panel, and the image assets we used for the buttons.
