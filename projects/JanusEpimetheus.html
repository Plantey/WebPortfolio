Janus & Epimetheus

Year: 2024 | Medium: 3D Animation

Project Summary

The Janus and Epimetheus Video is a 3D animation that accurately depicts the orbit-switching phenomena of two of Saturn's moons: Janus and Epimetheus. It was a small creative component for a larger assignment for an astronomy class. It was created using Maya for both the modeling and animation. Then the image seqeuence was brought to premiere for compilation and subtitles.
